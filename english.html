Study in Switzerland with FES

FES, Your Personal VIP Education Advisor in Switzerland

  • Experienced Consultant with first-hand experience in Swiss top private schools provides personalized advice and support for candidates
  • One-stop solution for your overseas education needs: providing holistic, complete and detailed support from school selection to onsite support in Switzerland throughout your study

Study in Switzerland – Your First Step into International and Exclusive Community

Swiss private schools are globally known for their high standards and quality of learning. Students seeking academic excellence from across 120 countries are welcomed each year by prestigious Swiss private schools renowned for their comprehensive and challenging learning programs. All Levels of education are available from kindergarten to University, including summer and winter camp as well as finishing schools from girls aged 16 and above.

About FES

Foreign Education Service (FES) is a specialized education agency providing holistic, complete and detailed support for Japanese students wishing to pursue overseas education in top private schools in Switzerland and in UK.

Closely working with each families understanding your values and visions, we provide detailed counseling and holistic support for your overseas education needs, from choosing the right school, to throughout your academic experience in Switzerland. We offer personalized advisory and consultancy service for each child, based on individual background, personality, and visions for future. Our strong and close network with Swiss international schools offers clients up-to-date information together with detailed advisory on Swiss education system and tradition, which only those who themselves went through the system in person can provide.

Our Service

Consultancy, assistance and support for candidates considering Swiss schools
  • Counseling, school selection, information services for each schools
  • English level assessment
Admission guidance and application support
  • Assistance on preparation of application forms and other necessary documents
  • Liaison with schools, supporting throughout the application process
Visa assistance, Travel assistance, Pre-departure guidance
Onsite support in Switzerland for school visits, interviews, etc
Assistance during education program
  • Essay coaching
  • Translation services for documents and parental communication
Other services
  • Marketing events in Japan for Swiss institutions inviting school representatives
  • Assistance in arranging private drivers for school tours

コンサルタントの紹介

Rui Kono

Swiss Educational Consultant

At the age of 11, Rui attended summer school in Summer Camp Montana. After one year of consideration studying abroad, she enrolled in St. George’s, one of the prominent public schools in Montreux. Having thrived within their education system, Rui successfully advanced and spent the next two years of high school at College du Leman. She continued her studies at the International University of Geneva, where she obtained her Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Business Art and Communication and during which time, she developed valuable professional skills through her internship at the United Nations. Rui worked at the currency trading company in Geneva, after graduating in 2004.

Now based in Switzerland, Rui provides local support service to Japanese cliental. She takes advantages of her Swiss education and broad network built over the past 12 years to provide comprehensive consulting service to Japanese cliental.

Mika Gokita

Swiss Educational Consultant

Mika joined Brillantmont International School’s summer course before enrolling in their academic year program for the American Diploma. While in college, Mika worked at FES as a Swiss Consultant for 2 years. After graduation, she worked in the IT industry as Marketing Specialist and Program Sales for 6 years, gaining professional marketing skills in a global environment. Now she works as a Swiss Consultant at FES and provides local support here in Tokyo Office.
